About Me

Nice to meet yah! I’m Jennifer and I’m a UX/UI designer. I’ve always enjoyed finding solutions to challenging problems and believe that is one core part of design. My design style tends to be simple and quirky, yet it’s still mindful, thoughtful, and based on research. Although that is my design style, I firmly believe it’s important to design for the user or client’s taste. In my free time you’ll find me researching dessert recipes, playing games, watching videos/series, singing, eating, or being a sloth.

I’m always happy to connect with newcomers! Feel free to fill out my contact form at the bottom of this page.

Soft Skills & Values

Humility

It’s not easy receiving criticism on your designs, but it teaches us to be more open-minded. Criticism shows different perspectives and it’s important to acknowledge them.

Teamwork

The best thing about teamwork is being able to draw in everyone’s specialties and forming a bond. One can only do so much, but a team has so much more potential.

Simplicity

Do you know the saying, “Work smarter, not harder”? Well, simplicity is the same thing. Complex solutions may work, but if you continue iterating or ideating, you are bound to find a simpler solution.

User-Centered

Design should always keep the user in mind. What is the point of a product with a wonderful solution if no one uses it?

Accessibility

Design needs to be inclusive. We may have a specific target audience, but everyone deserves to experience or receive information on the same level.
